Hot Spots

Seven Brothers Burgers

Originating on the Hawaiian Island of Oahu, This fresh take on American Classic food is a hit. You can't go wrong with their Shem Burger (Bacon Guacamole), with a side of fries and Nutella shake! make sure to try their homemade fry sause, it's to die for!

Maria Bonita's Mexican Grill

If you are in search of not only authentic mexican food, but also an authentic mexican experience, be sure to visit Maria Bonita's Mexican Grill in Orem. From their classic tacos to the enchiladas, you are sure to find a entree that will meet your below the border needs!

Gloria's Little Italy

Okay pasta lovers, here is the next place you need to try. Gloria's is an overall excellent experience with amazing classic Itallian dishes to great Itallian entertainment. Not to mention that their portions sizes are large enough to share, or to take home for a midnight snack.
